Tourist Routes

A-one-day tour of Dongguan Keyuan Garden, Weigu Village of Shipai Tang and Songshan Lake
Brief introduction of the route:
Visit Dongguan Keyuan Garden, one of the four famous gardens of Guangdong Province after breakfast. The designer of the garden integrates residence, drawing room, villa, courtyard, parterre, park and study together artistically, along with all the others such as Pavilions, halls, pavilions, landscape, bridges and courts. After lunch go to Weigu Village of Shipai Tang -national key cultural object protection unit and one of the most beautiful villages of Dongguan- by bus. All the buildings in the village are the ancient architectures with the feature of Ming and Qing Dynasty, including about 268 ancient dwellings, 21 ancestral halls, 19 libraries, 10 ancient wells, 4 surrounding gates and 28 blockhouses existing. With a rational overall arrangement, the ancient village is a featured agricultural village cultural landscape constituted by enclosures, blockhouses, lanes, ancestral halls, libraries, dwellings, ancient wells, ponds, ancient banyan trees and so on. On the way back to Dongguan visit Songshan Lake and see the top landscape “misty rain of Songshan Lake” of Dongguan. Return to hotel after dinner.
B. Hong Kong travel
Brief introduction of the route:
After breakfast, take special bus at the park of Jiahua Hotel to go to Shenzhen Bay Control Point. You will be sent to Hong Kong Harbor City after going through the custom and you can arrange your travel in Hong Kong freely.
In the evening during 19:00 to 20:00 buses will be arranged at the drop-off place to return to control point and you will be sent back to Dongguan Jiahua Hotel. Please be sure to reach the departure point on time, in order to prevent influencing your schedule of the second day due to the travel delay of the day.
Tips: *the factory will give a subsidy of 600 Hong Kong dollars per person; * please be sure to handle your one-way permit and endorsement in advance if you choose this route, and feedback the information to the affairs division, in order to prevent impact of your travel due to the documentation problem.
C-TEAM FAW TOYOTA 2013 golf friendly
Leave to *** golf course from Dongguan Jiahua Hotel after breakfast, and group all the guests according to average scores and begin matches, after which a prize-awarding ceremony will be held.
Introduction of the court: To be confirmed. Entries required:
Eligibility The average scores of the latest 10 matches or the recent half year is no more than 120 scores (special-case exception). Group chairman, general managers, FTMS related personnel and leaders of relevant business entities registering through WEB.。 Match regulations All the contestants should provide equipments (ball arms, gloves and gym shoes) for themselves, and please communicate with receptionists in advance when you check in if there is any special case.
