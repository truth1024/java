commercial activities routes

A. Hong Kong travel-Disney/ City Gate Outlets
Brief introduction of the route:
After breakfast, take special bus at the park of Jiahua Hotel to go to Shenzhen Bay Control Point. You will be sent to Hong Kong Disneyland and City Gate Outlets (either-or) after going through the custom. Hong Kong Disneyland is the fifth theme park constructed with Disneyland model around the world and is the 11th theme part of Disney around the world; City Gate Outlets is the only discount mall of well-known shops in Hong Kong, bringing you over 80 brands covering international fashion, sportswear, cosmetic, accessory, children’s wear and household items and providing you with a treasure hunt-like shopping enjoyment with at least 30% to 70% discount during the full year.
In the evening during 19:00 to 20:00 buses will be arranged respectively at the drop-off places of Hong Kong Disneyland and City Gate Outlets to return to control point and you will be sent back to Dongguan Jiahua Hotel. Please be sure to reach the departure point on time, in order to prevent influencing your schedule of the second day due to the travel delay of the day.
Tips:
*the factory will give a subsidy of 600 Hong Kong dollars per person, and the route doesn’t includes any meal arrangement which calls your sefl-care;
*please be sure to handle your one-way permit and endorsement in advance if you choose this route, and feedback the information to the affairs division, in order to prevent impact of your travel due to the documentation problem.

C. Shenzhen Overseas Chinese Town
Visit East Overseas Chinese Town of Dameisha, Shenzhen, which is invested 3.5 billion yuan by Overseas Chinese Town Group. Occupying an area of near 9 square kilometers, the park consists six major modules including Grand Canyon Ecological Park, Chaxi Valley Leisure Park, Yunhai Valley Sports Park, Huaxing Temple, theme hotels community and Tianlu Mansion. Visit the grand canyon after reaching the scenic spot, and experience the exciting fiord driftage, water battleship, house boat, mountain torrents and wooden roller coaster (two hours travel). Arrange lunch in due time and visit Chaxi Valley, feel the European flavored Interlaken Town, and be sent back to the hotel after finishing travel.
C-TEAM FAW TOYOTA 2013 golf friendly
Leave to Dongguan Guanlan Lake Club Olazabal Course from Dongguan Jiahua Hotel after breakfast, and group all the guests according to average scores and begin matches, after which a prize-awarding ceremony will be held.
Introduction of the course:
Olazabal has designed 15 coursed in Europe, and the Guanlan Lake is the first course he designed in China, with the design philosophy of sense of beauty, unique, harmony with environment and playability. With his own outstanding performance in bunker play aspect Olazabal designs a lot of large and deep bunkers for his course. Moreover, this course is the longest one among the ten courses of Guanlan Lake, reaching a length of 7,400 yards.
In this course even the best player will face a largest test. Natural groves and brooks construct beautiful backgrounds for many holes. From the special square service area and inflexed dallies grass lanes this real champion course can be distinguished from others. The bunkers are made up with many moulds with concave-convex edges, thick grass graves and bowl appearances, which must attract players’ attention. From the angle of playability, players will face choices at every hole, since it may fall from eagle to double bogey accidentally no matter you choose a safe and conservative path or a risky and appreciating path.
Entries required:
Eligibility: The average scores of the latest 10 matches or the recent half year is no more than 120 scores (special-case exception). Group chairman, general managers, FTMS related personnel higher than chief level and leaders of relevant business entities registering through WEB. Match regulations: All the contestants should provide equipments (ball arms, gloves and gym shoes) for themselves, and please communicate with receptionists in advance when you check in if there is any special case. All the contestants should comply with fixture, and those who can’t gather, set out and reach meeting place on time will be regarded as giving up the eligibility. Those prize winners who don’t attend prize-awarding ceremony without excuse will be regarded as giving up the prize, and will be alternated by prize winners selected by organizing committee. Clothing requirements: During the match all the contestants should wearing the uniform clothing provided by organizing committee- a cotton wind recovery vest, a T-shirt and a cap (they will be provided when you check in the hotel, and please provide casual pants and gym shoes for yourself). In order to guarantee the progress of the match, pleases wear the match wears in hotel before go to golf course by bus. Remarks： 1. For the convenience of staff’s identification, please use the travel bag made by the organizing committee uniformly to consign golf bag, and sign your name on the travel bag. 2. during this activity golf bags will be transported to golf course uniformly by the affairs division, and please carry your gym shoes to the golf course by yourself and change them in dressing rooms of the course.
